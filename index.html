WHY MACADAMIA NUTS?

Macadamia Nuts are one of the healthiest food items we can eat! They are packed with so many vitamins, minerals, healthy fats and oils, anti-oxidants, proteins, and more. The list goes on and on! Scientists are only just discovering the health benefits, but here at Niassa Nut Co, we are ahead of the curve.


These little nutrient-filled nuts are proven to help prevent heart disease and cancer, they're also good for your gut, and they even aid in weight loss. Here at the Niassa Factory, we have been exploring all the delicious ways in which we can make use of the macadamia nut. We have created the most mouth-watering, good-for-you products around.



ABOUT US

We are a family run business based in the beautiful province of Niassa in Mozambique. We have planted and nurtured over 1500 hectares of Macadamia Nut Trees over the years, currently producing over 5000 tons of Nut-In-Shell per year. We supply to all industries - large and small! We are able to accomodate orders of Nut-In-Shell in container loads for export, as well as smaller orders for local supply. We also manufacture our very own range of products using our Macs.
